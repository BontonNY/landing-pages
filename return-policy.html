If an item you ordered does not turn out to be what you expected, you may simply return it within 30-days from receipt for a refund. Refunds will be credited to the original form of payment for the net price after discounts and/or coupons. After 30-days, we shall process your return for a store credit. We will apply the following rules to process your return:

  • Returns are accepted for a refund within 30-days of receipt.
  • Merchandise should be in the same condition as when it was received and should be unworn, undamaged, unwashed, and sealable, with original tags attached, and all packaging materials included.
  • Beauty products may be returned if they are unopened, unused, and the original cellophane is fully intact.
  • We reserve the right, at our sole discretion, to determine if returned merchandise is in saleable condition.
  • If the return is after 30-days of receipt, then the refund is a store credit at the then applicable lowest sale price for the item returned.
  • A pickup and/or restock fee may apply for returning large/heavy merchandise.
  • All returned merchandise weighing 25 lbs. or more will incur a shipping fee of $10.99 for each occurrence.

Certain items are Non-Returnable

  • Altered apparel, special orders, cloth face mask coverings, perishable or personalized/monogrammed items, and items identified as "final sale" cannot be returned.
  • Clearance items
  • Furniture. If the item arrived damaged upon delivery, do not accept the delivery. If you accept the delivery, you shall be responsible for return shipping costs.
  • Individual parts of a Bundle: “2 for $50” unless you return the entire bundle
  • Items marked “Non-Returnable” on the sale page
  • Items that have already been assembled by you
  • Intimates such as lingerie, swimsuits and undergarments, etc.

Please note that customers are responsible for return shipping costs under some conditions. We will provide you a return shipping label as a courtesy, but under certain return reasons the cost will be deducted from your refund. We will only refund your shipping costs if:

  • The item was damaged in the initial shipping process;
  • An incorrect item was shipped by us; or
  • We shipped a defective item.

How to return?

To start your return, follow these 4 easy steps:

  • Step 1
    Click here and enter the Order Number and Zip Code from your Order Confirmation email. 
  • Step 2

Select the items, quantity, return reason, and include a picture or two of the items.

  • Step 3
    Place the unused items with all original packing materials, tags etc. in a box and seal it. 
  • Step 4
    Print and attach the shipping label on the outside of the box and drop it at a UPS location near you.

Missing packaging and/or tags etc. may delay or invalidate your returns, so please include them.  And don’t forget to remove or cover any existing shipping labels from the package.  You may track your return status here.
